Evaluate your organisation's readiness against ISO 30301:2019 — the international standard for Management Systems for Records. This assessment covers 35 evidence-based checkpoints across 7 domains, including context, leadership, planning, support, operations, records controls, and performance evaluation.

About ISO 30301:2019: ISO 30301 specifies requirements for a Management System for Records (MSR) — a systematic approach to creating, capturing, classifying, retaining, and disposing of records as evidence of business activities, decisions, and regulatory obligations. Annex A provides implementation controls for records processes, records controls, and records systems.
