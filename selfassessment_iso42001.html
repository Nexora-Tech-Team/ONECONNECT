Scope of this assessment: ISO/IEC 42001:2023 applies to any organisation — regardless of size, type, or nature — that provides or uses products or services that utilise AI systems. Each item requires two evidence checks:
  • Documented — a policy, procedure, register, plan, or approved record exists.
  • Implemented — operational evidence exists: logs, screenshots, audit trails, completed assessments, or review records.
