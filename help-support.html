How do I reset my password?
Go to Account Settings → Password & Security. Enter your current password, then set a new one. Your new password must be at least 8 characters with uppercase, lowercase, a number, and a special character. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.
How do I update my profile information?
Navigate to Account Settings → Personal Information. You can update your full name, job title, phone number, and profile photo there. Changes are saved automatically once you click "Save Changes".
How do I enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)?
Go to Account Settings → Two-Factor Authentication. Toggle the "Enable 2FA" switch, then scan the QR code with an authenticator app (such as Google Authenticator or Authy). Enter the 6-digit code to confirm. We strongly recommend enabling 2FA for all accounts.
Is my assessment progress saved automatically?
Yes. All self-assessment responses are saved to your browser's local storage in real time — every time you change a rating or add a note. You can leave the page and return later to continue exactly where you left off. To ensure data is not lost, avoid clearing your browser cache while an assessment is in progress.
What do the maturity ratings (0–4) mean?
The 0–4 scale follows standard GRC maturity levels: 0 — Not Implemented: No process in place. 1 — Planned: Planned but not yet executed. 2 — Partially Implemented: Some activities in place but inconsistent. 3 — Largely Implemented: Documented, applied, and mostly consistent. 4 — Fully Implemented: Complete, embedded, and continuously improved.
Can I export my assessment results?
Yes. Each self-assessment has an "Export Report" button that downloads a formatted PDF summary of your maturity scores, radar chart, and domain breakdown. This can be shared with management or included in audit evidence packages.
How do I download my certificate?
Go to My Certifications. Find your certificate card and click the "Download PDF" button. The original certificate file will be downloaded to your device. You can also click the preview thumbnail to open a full-size view before downloading.
What should I do if my certificate shows "Expired"?
An expired certificate means your certification period has lapsed. Click "Renew Certification" on the certificate card to begin a new certification cycle. You will need to complete a surveillance audit or full re-certification depending on your certificate type. Contact our team for a renewal timeline.
Where can I find my invoice or subscription details?
Billing information is managed by your organisation's account administrator. If you need an invoice, please contact your administrator or raise a support ticket selecting "Billing Enquiry" as the category. Enterprise clients receive invoices via email from accounts@cbqaglobal.com.
Can I add multiple users to my organisation account?
Yes. Multi-user access is available on Team and Enterprise plans. Organisation admins can manage user seats from Account Settings → Organisation. Additional user seats beyond your plan limit will be billed at the per-seat rate on your next invoice cycle.
