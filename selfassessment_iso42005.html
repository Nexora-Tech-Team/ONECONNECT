About ISO/IEC 42005:2025
ISO/IEC 42005:2025 provides guidance for conducting AI System Impact Assessments (ASIA) — a structured process to identify and evaluate the potential effects of an AI system on individuals, organizations, and society. This self-assessment covers two main areas:
  • ASIA Process (Clause 5) — How the organization establishes, integrates, scopes, governs, and monitors its ASIA process.
  • ASIA Documentation (Clause 6) — What the ASIA record should contain: AI system description, data, algorithms, interested parties, and impact analysis.
